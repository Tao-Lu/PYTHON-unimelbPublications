What can you do with this site

The overview page gives a dashboard look across entire department. This include the statistic of cooperation with affiliation within Australia, total number of publication each year from 1979, popularity of topic...

If you are unfamiliar with Unimelb Computing and Information System department, simply view our Author page to explore all of the staff.

To search a particular item:
  • Enter a name in the search bar, and choose one from all the result that match the search term. Simply click on the author name to view its details.

  • Enter a title or some part of a title to search the paper. Click on the search result to view the detail of the paper.

  • Enter some keywords in the search bar to search for relevant papers. Click on the search result to view the detail of the paper.

    • Author Detail:
